Common Questions

Everything you need
to know

Is Zolvyn AI a substitute for a real lawyer? +
No. Zolvyn AI is an educational tool that helps you understand Indian law, analyze documents, and prepare for legal situations. For serious legal matters, always consult a qualified advocate. Zolvyn helps you walk into that meeting informed.
How accurate is the Legal Q&A and Case Predictor? +
The Legal Q&A uses RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) — it retrieves answers directly from Indian law documents, so it's grounded in real legal text. The Case Predictor uses AI reasoning based on the facts you provide. Both are highly informative but should be treated as guidance, not guaranteed outcomes.
Is my data private and secure? +
Yes. We do not store your contract PDFs, case descriptions, or personal information on our servers. All analysis is processed in real-time and discarded after your session. Your legal matters stay private.
Which areas of Indian law does Zolvyn cover? +
Zolvyn covers a wide range of Indian law including IPC (criminal law), consumer protection, property disputes, employment law, family law, contract law, RTI, cheque bounce cases (NI Act), and more. The knowledge base is continuously updated.
Can I use the generated documents in court? +
Zolvyn-generated documents follow standard Indian legal formats and are a strong starting point. However, we recommend having any document reviewed by a qualified lawyer before using it in a legal proceeding or signing it.
Do I need to create an account to use Zolvyn? +
The free plan requires no account — just open the app and start using it. For Pro and Business plans, a simple signup with email is required to manage your subscription.
